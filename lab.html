Technical Specifications

Software

  • Krita
  • DaVinci Resolve

Parameters

  • 1080p Resolution
  • 30 FPS
  • 1 Minute Duration

Step-by-Step Process

Step 1

Select Reference Video from Youtube

Step 2

Cut Reference Video into 5 Second Clips via "Individual Clips" on DaVinci Resolve

Step 3

Import Video Animation onto Krita and Trace Per Frame (30FPS mandatory)

Step 4

Render and Compile all Rotoscoped Clips in DaVinci Resolve

Step 5

Edit Elements using DaVinci Resolve (e.g. lyrics, background, chroma key)

Step 6

Upload onto Youtube!
